 FAG 2309-TVH Self-aligning ball bearings are open design double row, self-retaining units comprising outer rings with a concave raceway, inner rings with a cylindrical bore and ball and glass fibre reinforced polyamide cage assemblies. Sealed design FAG 2209-2RS-TVH are also available.
